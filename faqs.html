WHAT IS TECHNICAL ANALYSIS?

Technical analysis is a trading methodology used to analyze investment and locate trading opportunities by examining statistical trends extracted from trading activities, such as price movements and volume. In comparison to fundamental analysts who seek to determine/evaluate a security's intrinsic value, technical analysts concentrate on trends of price movements, market signals and numerous other analytical charts.

WHAT IS ALGORITHM TRADING?

Algorithmic trading is a method of executing orders using automated pre-programmed trading instructions for variables such as time, price and volume. This method of trading has been developed to make use of the speed and data processing advantages that computers have over human traders. 

WHAT IS A TRADING STRATEGY?

A trading strategy is a method of buying and selling in markets that is based on predefined rules that are used to make trading decisions.
